EquiLearn is a revolutionary platform designed to make education accessible for children with various disabilities, including visual, hearing, and sensory impairments.

About EquiLearn

EquiLearn is a revolutionary learning platform specifically designed to make education accessible for children with various disabilities. We believe every child deserves equal access to quality education, regardless of their physical or cognitive challenges.

Our platform incorporates multiple accessibility features to ensure all children can learn effectively. To get started, simply speak your commands or use the navigation menu. Our AI assistant is always ready to help explain any concept or guide you through the platform.
